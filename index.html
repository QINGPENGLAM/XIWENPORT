TITLE : Time


MEDIUM: Acrylic


Date : December 2022


Description : The huge circle forms an incomplete or broken clock. Iron, the silver-gray lines that wrote XI and XII, the roman number of the final two digits of the day, bonded and restricted two figures, my past laying, and my future standing. The purple dark colors show the complex mood and confusion about whether excited or fearful. They were both stuck and unpleasant with the present, they blocked their eyes, stressed toward the unknown, and couldn’t embrace themselves. It’s self-illusion, self-lies. Some objects in the images, including the Christmas tree in the right-down corner, and the buildings in the background, are related to the current, like the reality in surrealism. The flowers behind Future that represent death, the process of fresh to wilt, further demonstrated the topic of time. The composition of letting the top half be heavier aims to fulfill the mood of uncertainty, where the steps couldn’t be conformed. However, time themselves formed a certain shape obviously in the middle of the canvas: a triangle, it is my hope and my wish for myself to walk beyond.
